Welcome to Our Trust. Unity, Kindness, Happiness...A trust is a type of non-governmental organization(NGO) that works towards charitable and social welfare causes in India. Both trusts and NGOs are eligible for tax exemptions under the Income Tax Act.Here are some differences between trusts and NGOs:Structure .A trust is a legal arrangement where a trustee holds and manages assets or property for the benefit of a beneficiary. An NGO is a voluntary organization that operates independently of the government.Focus.Trusts focus on managing and distributing assets for the benefit of specific beneficiaries. NGOs workunder a broad mandate to bring about constructive change in society through projects, programs, andadvocacy efforts.Formation Trusts and NGOs differ in many ways like the formation process and documentation. NGOs are registered under three different legislations depending on the type of NGO.
